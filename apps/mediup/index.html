How it works
Up and running in 30 seconds.
1

Install from Chrome Web Store

Click "Add to Chrome" — no account or sign-up required.

2

Pin MEDIUP to your toolbar

Click the puzzle icon 🧩 and pin MEDIUP for quick access.

3

Play any media and open MEDIUP

Open YouTube, Spotify or any video site, start playing, then click the MEDIUP icon.

4

Set your timers or schedule

Pick a stop time, resume time, or schedule a URL to play at any hour.
