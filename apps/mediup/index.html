How it works
Up and running in seconds.
1

Install MEDIUP

Get the Android app from Google Play, or add the Chrome extension from the Web Store.

2

Grant notification access (Android)

MEDIUP reads your current playing track via the notification listener. On Chrome, just pin the extension.

3

Play any media

Open Spotify, YouTube, VLC or any media app — MEDIUP detects what's playing automatically.

4

Set your timers or schedule

Pick a stop time, start time, or schedule a URL to open at any hour.
