Features

Everything you need to track your time

⚡

One-tap Tracking

Start any activity in a single tap. Switch between activities instantly. The timer runs silently in the background.

🔀

Parallel Mode

Track two things at once — a podcast while cooking, music while working. Parallel sessions stay separate in analytics.

➕

Forgot to Track?

Log any past time block manually. The app intelligently adjusts overlapping sessions in your history.

📊

Smart Analytics

Pie charts and breakdowns for today, this week, or this month. See exactly where your hours go.

📄

PDF Export

Generate a clean activity report and share it via email, save to files, or print — in seconds.

🎨

Fully Customisable

Create your own activities with custom names, icons, and colours. Organise them into groups.
